Group Taks

Manage your team productivity

Overview

This is an app that lets you register tasks and groups in two levels, allowing one class of Users (managers) to keep track and assign tasks to other levels (employees). It also go beyond that, allowing a direct communication between them with a live-chat.

Features

This app has a built-in live-chat that provides easy communication between you and your team, besides a timer to allow everyone that register the task to keep the application in the background counting the the time you need to do a task.
